About Shri Sai Baba

🕌

Universal Saint

Sai Baba transcended all religious boundaries — a living embodiment that all paths lead to the one God, revered by Hindus and Muslims alike.

❤️

Love for All

He spent his life feeding the poor, healing the sick, and comforting the troubled. His door was open to every person regardless of caste or creed.

🔥

Sacred Udi

Baba's sacred Dhuni fire burned continuously at Dwarkamai. Its ash — Udi — is believed to carry healing power and is distributed to devotees worldwide.

📿

Eternal Guru

Revered as a Sadguru, devotees across generations experience his living grace through answered prayers, timely guidance, and divine protection.
